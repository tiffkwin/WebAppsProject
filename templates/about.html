ABOUT THE PROJECT

In order to create a project that fully utilizes the skills and tools that we have learned within this course, our group will be building a web application that prompts a user for their date of birth and then provides them with a personalized summary which includes the generational group that they are a part of (Baby Boomer, Millenial, Gen Z, etc.), their horoscope, their lunar zodiac, and other pieces of information that are specific to the date on which they were born. This web application will be engaging and informative for users.

During a period where social distancing is being enforced and encouraged, this application could provide much needed entertainment to all the individuals who find themselves bored and stuck in self-quarantine. In order to begin developing this project, we will need to do a bit of research and first collect the information that we will eventually be providing to the users of the system. We will also need to be able to design an application that is intuitive and fun to use. In addition, we could provide links to resources regarding best hygiene practices and recommendations from the CDC regarding COVID-19 to ensure that the mission of the application is even further achieved.
